Our Campus

Offically opened in October 2009, University Centre Peterborough offers a range of different courses based on different subject areas and taught by tutors who have experince working in those fields.

About us

University Centre Peterborough mission is 'To engage, enable and empower students to meet the challenges of the future'

Students are also the heart of the University and are involved in the decision making process via the student council and student representatives.

Learning Resources

Students are able to access most learning resources remotely, through Canvas and Anglia Ruskin's digital library

Emails sent from the University to students are sent via student emails which can be accesed from outside of the building. It is recommended that students check their emails on a regular basis.
